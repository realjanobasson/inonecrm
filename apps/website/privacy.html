Privacy Policy

This page explains how InOneCRM handles information you share when you use our website, trial signup, and contact forms.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone/WhatsApp, company).
  • Messages you send through our contact form.
  • Basic usage data (like device/browser details) for security and performance.

How we use it

  • To respond to your questions and schedule onboarding.
  • To provide the trial experience and improve the product.
  • To protect against spam, abuse, and fraud.

Sharing

We don’t sell your information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us run the site (hosting, email delivery, analytics) when needed to deliver the service.

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect information, but no system is 100% secure. Please avoid sending sensitive information through the contact form.

Your choices

  • You can ask us what information we have about you.
  • You can request corrections or deletion where appropriate.
  • You can opt out of non‑essential communication at any time.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@inonecrm.com.
